University of Professional Studies,Accra

University of Professional Studies,Accra is very prominent university in Ghana where I took my first degree program. I studied Bachelor of Science in Information Technology Management. It is well known for its professionalism and its specialty in business related programs. It is located at Madina a suburb in Accra.
